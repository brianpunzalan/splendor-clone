Splendor — Gem Merchants : Asset Credits
========================================

This project is an original, fan-made clone of the board game *Splendor*
(designed by Marc André, published by Space Cowboys). It is non-commercial and
made for learning. "Splendor" and its card/noble data are the intellectual
property of their respective owners; this clone reimplements the rules with
original code and openly-licensed artwork.

  - "Cut diamond" by Lorc            — used for gem tokens & cards

  - "Two coins" by Delapouite        — used for gold (joker) tokens

The icons were recolored and recomposed via CSS for this game. The original
